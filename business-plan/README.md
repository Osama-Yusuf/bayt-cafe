## Market Snapshot (2025-2026)

- Egypt's coffee consumption **doubled** in 5 years (36K tons → 70K tons)
- Egypt is among the **top 3 fastest-growing** coffee markets in MENA by outlet count
- ~60% of Egypt's 110M population projected to reach middle-class by 2025
- Younger Egyptians (under 35) view coffee as a **lifestyle signal**, not a commodity
- Specialty coffee scene in Cairo is **booming** — Brown Nose, 30 North, The Roastery, Brew'd are leading the wave
- Egypt Coffee Festival 2025 attracted **World Barista Champions**
