# 04 — Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

Position between Cilantro (mass-market) and ultra-premium (Brown Nose). You're selling **specialty quality at accessible prices** — better than chains, but not intimidating.

(Industry standard COGS for coffee beverages: 22-28%)

| Drink | Ingredient Cost | Sell Price | Margin |
|-------|----------------|-----------|--------|
| Espresso (double) | ~8 EGP | 45 EGP | 82% |

| Latte | ~16 EGP (beans + milk) | 70 EGP | 77% |

## Pricing Tactics

### 1. Anchor Pricing
Place your most expensive drink (specialty pour-over at 100 EGP) at the top of the menu. Everything below it looks reasonable by comparison.

### 2. Size Upselling
Offer Regular and Large for key drinks. The "large" costs you ~5 EGP more in ingredients but you charge 15-20 EGP more. This is your highest-margin move.

| | Regular | Large | Your Extra Cost | Extra Revenue |
|---|---------|-------|----------------|---------------|
| Latte | 70 | 85 | ~5 EGP (more milk) | +15 EGP |
| Iced Latte | 80 | 95 | ~6 EGP | +15 EGP |
| Americano | 55 | 65 | ~2 EGP (more water) | +10 EGP |

### 3. Add-On Charges

| Add-On | Charge | Your Cost |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Extra shot | +15 EGP | ~4 EGP |
| Flavor syrup | +10 EGP | ~2 EGP |
| Oat milk upgrade | +15 EGP | ~5 EGP |
| Whipped cream | +10 EGP | ~2 EGP |

### 4. Combo / Bundle

Offer a "coffee + pastry" combo at a slight discount:
- Latte + Croissant: 70 + 40 = 110 → **Combo: 95 EGP**
- Customer feels they got a deal; you still make ~65 EGP margin on a 95 EGP sale

### 5. No Discount Culture

Do not start with discounts or promotions that train customers to expect low prices. Your differentiator is quality and experience, not price. If you want to drive traffic at launch, do:
- Free tasting events (costs you very little, builds word of mouth)
- "First coffee free" on opening day
- Loyalty card (buy 7, get 1 free) — this rewards repeat customers, not bargain hunters

## Price Review Cadence

Review prices every **6 months** in Egypt due to inflation and currency fluctuation:

- Check supplier price changes (beans, milk, disposables)
- Recalculate margins
- Compare with competitors
- Adjust in small increments (5-10 EGP) — don't shock customers with big jumps
- Always adjust upward with a reason customers can see: "We've upgraded to a higher-scoring single origin" — price increase justified by quality increase

## Loyalty Program

### Simple Stamp Card (Start Here)
- Buy 7 drinks → 8th free
- Cost to you: ~16 EGP per free latte every 8 visits = 2 EGP per visit
- The free drink drives the 7 paid visits that wouldn't otherwise happen

### Digital Loyalty (Phase 2)
- Move to app-based (Bonat or similar Egyptian loyalty platform)
- Points per purchase, redeemable for drinks/food
- Birthday rewards
- Push notifications for new items / slow days

Do NOT over-complicate loyalty at launch. A paper stamp card works perfectly for Month 1-6.
