## Revenue Targets to Break Even

Working backwards from costs:

| Scenario | Monthly Cost | Daily Revenue Needed (30 days) | Avg Ticket 80 EGP → Customers/Day |
|----------|-------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Lean | 110,000 | 3,700 | ~46 customers |
| Mid-Range | 175,000 | 5,800 | ~73 customers |
| Premium | 275,000 | 9,200 | ~115 customers |

These are break-even numbers. For profit, add 20-30% on top.

---

## Cash Reserve Recommendation

**Do not open with only enough for startup costs.** You need operating capital for the first 6-12 months while building a customer base.

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Startup costs | 1,000,000 – 2,500,000 EGP |
| 6-month operating reserve | 660,000 – 1,200,000 EGP |
| **Total Capital Needed** | **1,660,000 – 3,700,000 EGP** |

The operating reserve is what separates cafés that survive from those that don't. Most cafés are not profitable until month 6-18.
