Pricing Psychology

  • Anchor pricing: Put your most expensive drink first on the menu. Everything else feels reasonable.
  • Size upsell: Regular to Large costs you ~5 EGP more. Charge 15 EGP more. 200% markup on the upsell.
  • Add-ons: Oat milk +15, extra shot +15, syrup +10. Small amounts add up fast.
  • No discount culture: Never discount your coffee. Give a free cookie instead. Discounts train customers to wait for sales.

Highest Margin Plays

  • Cold Brew -- highest margin drink on the menu. Push it hard in summer.
  • Americano -- cheap to make, high perceived value. Staff should recommend it.
  • Tea -- extremely low cost. Good for non-coffee drinkers who come with groups.
  • Combo deals -- Latte + Croissant: 110 EGP to 95 EGP combo. Customer feels a deal, you still make ~65 EGP margin.

Ramadan Operations Guide 🌙
One month that can define your quarter. Plan it like a separate business.

⏱ Hours & Schedule

Recommended: Close 2PM-6PM (dead hours), open 6PM-2AM (prime time).
After Iftar (sunset to 2-3 AM): PEAK hours. Extended evening hours are essential.
Before Iftar: Most cafes close or do takeaway only for non-fasting customers.
Suhoor (3-4 AM): Some cafes stay open. Evaluate if your location has demand.
Staff shifts need complete restructuring for this month.

🍽 Special Ramadan Menu

Must-haves: Dates + Arabic coffee, Sahlab Latte, Qamar el-Din, Tamr Hindi, Karkade.
Ramadan combo: Dates + Arabic coffee + mini kunafa -- special pricing.
Heavier pastries: Kunafa, Qatayef, Basbousa -- partner with a bakery or make simple versions.
Reduce cold drinks (less demand during cooler evening hours).
Traditional drinks: Add Tamr Hindi (tamarind) and Karkade (hibiscus) -- classic Ramadan favorites.

👥 Staffing Adjustments

Shift structure: Split shifts or evening-only. Provide iftar meal for staff (mandatory).
Consider 1 temp part-time worker for the rush. Be flexible -- fasting while working is hard.

📣 Marketing Opportunities

Decor: Lanterns (fanous), crescent, warm lighting. Transform the space.
"Iftar at your cafe" -- position as a gathering spot. Double loyalty stamps.
Social media: Ramadan content calendar, daily greetings, special menu highlights.
Charity collaboration: Partner with a charity for "suspended coffee" (pay-it-forward). Great for brand and sawab.

💰 Financial Impact

Net effect: Usually neutral to slightly positive if planned well. Lower daytime, much higher evening.
Eid al-Fitr: MASSIVE day. Plan a special event and stock up in advance.

  • ◆ Pay zakat in the lunar month it becomes due. Many pay in Ramadan for extra reward.
  • ◆ Zakat can be given to local Egyptian charities, orphanages, or directly to those in need.
